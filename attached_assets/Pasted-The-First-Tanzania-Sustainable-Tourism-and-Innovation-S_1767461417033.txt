The First Tanzania Sustainable Tourism and Innovation Summit (TaSTIS) 2025.


TaSTIS is an annual platform that brings together tourism industry leaders, innovators, policymakers, and change makers from Tanzania, the wider East African region, and beyond. This inaugural summit will serve as a launchpad for building long-term dialogue, collaboration, and innovation toward a sustainable and inclusive tourism future.

Promote Sustainable Tourism
Sustainable tourism is not just a trend; it's a vital approach to preserving our planet for future generations. By choosing sustainable practices, we minimize our impact on the environment while enriching travel experiences and supporting local communities.

Why Sustainable Tourism Matters:
Environmental Preservation: Protect natural habitats, reduce pollution, and conserve biodiversity.
Education and Awareness: Raise awareness about environmental and social issues to foster responsible travel behavior.
Join the movement! Choose eco-friendly accommodations, support local artisans, and take part in conservation. Together, we build a sustainable future.
